How It Works

3 possible results.
Always clear next steps.

VetReady analyzes your pet's symptoms and gives you one of three outcomes — no guessing, no panic.
🚨
Go to Emergency Now
Serious or life-threatening signs detected. Head to an emergency clinic immediately — time matters.
📞
Call Your Vet Today
Symptoms need professional attention within the day. Call your vet to get seen or get advice.
🏠
Monitor at Home
No immediate red flags. Watch closely over 12–24 hours and recheck if anything changes.
