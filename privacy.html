VetReady

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

VetReady ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

VetReady is designed to work entirely on your device. We collect minimal information:

  • Pet profile data (name, species, breed, age, weight, medications, vaccines) — stored locally on your device only
  • Symptom check history — stored locally on your device only
  • Location data — used only when you tap "Use My Location" to find nearby vets; never stored or transmitted to us

How We Use Your Information

All pet profile and health data stays on your device. We do not transmit it to our servers, sell it, or share it with third parties.

When you use the Vet Finder, the app contacts OpenStreetMap's public API to retrieve nearby clinic locations. This request may include your approximate location or ZIP code. We do not store or log this data.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard browser/app storage (localStorage). Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

Third-Party Services

The Vet Finder feature uses OpenStreetMap and its Nominatim geocoding service. Please review OpenStreetMap's privacy policy for details on how they handle location queries.

Children's Privacy

VetReady is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@getvetready.app.
